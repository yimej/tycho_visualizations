    We utilized a pre compiled data set containing only information on domestic West Nile cases. We abbreviated the raw data to only include data starting from 2010. Records before 2010 contained too many instances of missing data and missing reporting periods. This dataset also does not clarify whether the case was domestically obtained or imported from a traveler coming back into the country.
